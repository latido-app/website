Latido helps you track the progress of your students.

Latido user progress files are small, uniquely identified, and encrypted to ensure that students are doing their own work.

Progress Report Generator

This program reads a whole folder full of .latido progress file and generates detailed reports about student progress and work time.

Download the latest release for your operating system.

Customize

Not satisfied with the built-in module? Want to use Latido to help students practice their choir or band music?

Read a tutorial on creating your own modules. All you need are MIDI files!
